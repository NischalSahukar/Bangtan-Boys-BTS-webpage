BTS - PIED PIPER

"Pied Piper" is a song by BTS. It was released on September 18, 2017, and appears as the fifth track for their fifth mini album Love Yourself: Her.
Based on the story of The Pied Piper, BTS presents a song where people listen to their music and simply can’t stop. This describes the behaviour of some saesang ARMYs and this might lead to the song being a diss track. An alternative interpretation might be the fact that BTS' songs are so enjoyable, which they have won many awards for, that they just had to make a song about it. If you look at the album series, it is ‘HER’. This might infer that the girl who they are talking about is addicted to their music and only loves them because of how addicting they are. BTS are practically ‘taking over’ the girl. This event will later teach them to love yourself if no one else properly will. This track is about the fans of BTS following them around but then also showing their gratitude.
